Moving from the Hedging Environments to the Market Wide Gamma Squeezes, let’s start out

First, let’s acknowledge that GTM seems to be done detecting negative gamma flows heading
towards the market – perhaps a bump in gamma squeeze counts in the next few days is
reasonable to expect, but per GTM it shouldn’t be long-lasting.
Additionally, it appears that the market routinely experiences “spikes” of short gamma flowing
towards the market (technically its an increase in the relative proportion of the rate of change of
short-gamma-growth weighted by net gamma – don’t worry, there will be an update to the

These spikes are interesting, namely because it seems that there is a dependency with the
current, overall environment of the gamma-flow (GTM-EMA) and the expected outcome from
these gamma-flow spikes.
