same engine. Institutionalize independent cross-checks:

   engine's execution code, matched on n/WR/PF within ~10% (existing CLAUDE.md rule

   engine without a KNOWABILITY.md row (stamp meaning, knowable-at, live emission,
   evidence). Template in KNOWABILITY.md.

   diff of live fills vs the engine's same-day backtest (the trade-audit tool
   exists); alert at >10% composition or WR divergence. The June "live
   underperformance = plumbing" investigations were this signal, unheeded.
