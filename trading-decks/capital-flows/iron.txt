WHY YOU CAN'T
EVERY PAIR STARVES THE THIRD
EDGE + FREQUENCY → you trade your best setups constantly, and risk capacity collapses .
FREQUENCY + RISK CAPACITY → you stay active and safe, but the edge per trade thins out .
EDGE + RISK CAPACITY → you keep the sharp, protected trades, so frequency drops hard .
Pick any two to push. The market sends the bill to the one you ignored.
