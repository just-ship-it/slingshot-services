THE REAL TEST
A BUBBLE TESTS WHO YOU ARE
YOUR LANE — your process, your size
the shiny detour
dead end
A mania makes the thing you've built feel small. The pull to drop your lane and chase it feels like growth — it's distraction. Trade it if you trade it, but as yourself — never the reckless version the mania wants you to become.
HOW MUCH AGGRESSION HAVE YOU EARNED?
SIZE IS A STAIRCASE — NOT A LEAP
$2k
step back
$40k+
THE LEAP — you get humbled
Push your size up a rung. Sit until it feels normal. Push again — sometimes stepping back. That's your nervous system learning to hold the heat. The mania tempts you to leap — skip rungs and you get humbled. Psychologically, not just financially.
Capacity is built, not given
THE PARADOX
AGGRESSIVE — INSIDE THE LINES
Size to conviction Big is fine. Big because you're excited is not.
Fixed risk per day Aggression lives in the size — never in dropping the stop.
Add to winners only Pay for new risk with locked-in profit .
Exit pre-written Decide where you leave while you're calm .
YOUR PLAYBOOK BREAKS
BUBBLE VOL ≠ NORMAL VOL
NORMAL · MEAN-REVERTING
Fade the extremes. Vol hands you reversion.
BUBBLE · ONE-WAY TREND
Dips stay shallow, shorts get run. Fading = death.
Sell strength here and you're the fuel.
HOW IT DIES
THE TOP & THE TRAPS
TELLS IT'S ENDING
▸ vertical blow-off ▸ everyone's an expert ▸ good news stops working ▸ "new paradigm" talk ▸ breadth narrows, leverage maxed
FATAL MISTAKES
▸ married the position ▸ shorted too early ▸ levered into illiquidity ▸ right thesis, wrong size ▸ gave it all back
MAKE PEACE WITH IT
YOU'RE GOING TO MESS IT UP
too early
the anomaly
too late
The trader who nails the exact top, exits clean, and walks off legendary? That's the anomaly — not the plan. Almost everyone is too early or too late.
Accept that going in and you can sit with any outcome — which is exactly what lets you hold your process while the move is screaming at you to abandon it.
THE WORK
DON'T CALL THE TOP. KEEP THE GAINS .
Rent the trend. Bank it in pieces. Bubbles punish the married and the late — not the disciplined.
