THE EVENT · LIFTOFF
Big lead-up, all the attention — retail, institutions, the whole tape locked on. Pick a real moment you were actually in. Today we'll use this one. Put it on the table and actually look at it.
DEBRIEF · STEP ONE
BEFORE: WHAT WAS THE PLAN?
01 WALKING IN
How did you feel walking in — calm, hyped, anxious, FOMO?
Did you have an actual plan — or were you just there because everyone else was?
Why were you participating — or why weren't you? And were you doing it for you , or for someone else?
Was the vehicle, horizon, and risk defined up front — or decided on the fly?
Be honest about the version of you that showed up before the first tick printed.
DEBRIEF · STEP TWO
DURING: WERE YOU EXECUTING — OR REACTING?
02 IN THE MOMENT
When and why did you actually pull the trigger — if you did at all?
How did it feel while you were in it?
Were you executing a plan — or reacting to the screen?
Did it shock you? Or had you run so many redundancy plans that nothing surprised you ?
Your reactivity in the moment is the tell. War-gamed traders don't flinch — they recognize.
