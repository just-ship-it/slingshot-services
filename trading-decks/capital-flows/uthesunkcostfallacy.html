THE WATER'S MOVING FASTER THAN EVER

VIOLENT ROTATIONS. RIP CURRENTS.

Leadership flips in weeks, not years now — so in water moving this fast, the one thing you can't afford is to be anchored in place.
FIRST — GET THE DEFINITION CLEAN

WHAT'S ALREADY SPENT IS GONE.

What you've already poured in is gone the second it's spent — all that matters now is where the capital goes from here.
WHY IT'S SO HARD TO LET GO

YOU STOP HOLDING THE POSITION. YOU BECOME IT.

Once the trade becomes a flag you've planted, selling feels like admitting you were wrong — so you defend the identity instead of the capital, and that's the trap.
LISTEN FOR THESE — THEY'RE ALL THE SAME TELL

EVERY EXCUSE IS AN ANCHOR IN DISGUISE.

⚓
"I'm already in too deep to sell now."
⚓
"I just need to get back to breakeven."
⚓
"It'll come back — it always does."
⚓
"I've done too much research to be wrong."
⚓
"Averaging down lowers my cost basis."
⚓
"I can't take the loss right now."
None of these is a thesis — each is just the money you've already spent reaching forward to make the next call, and that's the voice to stop trusting.
EXAMPLE ONE · THE ROTATION THEY MISSED

STUCK IN CRYPTO WHILE AI CAPEX RAN.

Anchored to a crypto identity, plenty couldn't rotate into the AI-capex trade flowing right past them — the thesis wasn't the problem, the attachment was.
EXAMPLE TWO · WAITING FOR THE CATASTROPHE

OIL BULLS BETTING ON A SHOCK THAT DEFLATED.

Convinced the oil shock had to come, they tied up capital waiting for a catastrophe that never arrived instead of putting it where the move actually was.
EXAMPLE THREE · THE ONE EVERYBODY DOES

ADDING TO THE LOSER TO REACH BREAKEVEN.

"I'm down too much to sell" — so you throw good money after bad chasing a breakeven the market never promised, when the real cost is everything that capital could've been.
THE BILL YOU NEVER SEE ON A STATEMENT

THE REAL PRICE IS THE PATH YOU GAVE UP.

Holding the loser costs you everything that capital could've become elsewhere — a gap that compounds silently, never a line item until it's enormous.
THE ONE QUESTION THAT CUTS THROUGH IT

WOULD YOU BUY IT TODAY — FROM SCRATCH?

If that position landed in your lap as fresh cash today and you wouldn't buy it, you're not "holding" — you're making a buy you'd never actually make.
WHO ACTUALLY COMPOUNDS

THE NIMBLE RIDE THE CURRENT. THE ANCHORED DRAG.

The outsized returns go to those who pivot before the attachment sets in — treating capital as a tool for the best forward EV.
