THE PLAYBOOK
THE OFF-SWITCH PROTOCOL
Run it every night · no exceptions
01 Stop P&L tab closed. Charts closed. The decision to stop is its own trade.
02 Put it away Phone in another room. Notifications off. Out of sight = out of loop.
03 Reset Ten minutes off the screen — walk, water, write tomorrow's setup.
04 Sleep Fixed window. 7–9 hours. No phone bedside. This is the position.
Don't try to use willpower at midnight when your prefrontal cortex is offline.
Build the architecture once, in daylight, when you're a different person. Then let it run you when the version of you that can't be trusted shows up.
